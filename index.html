About this blog

Life is messy, loud, and rarely follows a script.

I write about the parts that don't make it into the highlight reel. The conversation overheard on a bus. The well-timed mistake that turns out to be the whole point. The irony that shows up uninvited and refuses to leave.

Every interaction is a plot point. Every person is a character. Every day is a draft. I spend my time here deconstructing the world around me; not to make sense of it, exactly, but to find the narrative threads that tie us together when we're not looking.

This is a space for short stories, and the occasional realization that life is far more ironic than we give it credit for. No polished advice. No highlight reels. Just a story that feels like a conversation over a cold drink.
